The Origin

My father handed me Python during a gap year. I was supposed to study medicine. Instead I built a flashcard app during the same exam season it was meant to help me survive. I have not stopped building since.

02
The Method

Every project started as a real problem I noticed — people who couldn't afford a dermatologist, students drowning in announcements, Nigerians with skills and no marketplace. I built for those people.

Core belief
Technology built for the edges of the market is the most important kind. I see different problems because I live in a different context. That is not a limitation. It is an advantage.
